Ducati Panigale V4

The Panigale V4 is Ducati's first large-production street bike with a V4 engine. The Ducati Panigale V4 is a sport bike with a 1,103 cc (67.3 cu in) desmodromic 90° V4 engine introduced by Ducati in 2018 as the successor to the V-twin engined 1299. The development of the Panigale V4 started with the 2015 MotoGP engine.

Ducati Supersport S

The Ducati SuperSport is a series of air-cooled four stroke desmodromic 2-valve 90° V-twin motorcycles made by Ducati since 1988. A limited edition SuperSport called the SuperLight was sold in 1992. The name harked back to the round case 1973 Ducati 750 Super Sport, and the 1975 square case 750 and 900 Super Sport.
